PWM specifications

There are 8 PWM channels available on CFA10036 board.

Pinout Table

PWM channel no Arduino Uno Pin no Ball no of i.MX28 Pin no in CFA10037
PWM0 3 K7 P3.16
PWM1 5 L7 P3.17
PWM2* 6 K8 P3.18
PWM3* 9 G7 P3.20
PWM4* 10 E10 P3.29
PWM5* 11 F7 P3.22
PWM6* - E7 P3.23
PWM7 - E8 P3.26

* There is an issue with these PWMs. They won't work with Userspace code directly. Work around for that problem is available.
